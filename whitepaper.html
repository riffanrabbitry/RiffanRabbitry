Abstract

This whitepaper outlines the technical specifications, tokenomics, and implementation strategy for the RFN (Riffan Rabbit Token), a BEP-20 utility token on the BNB Smart Chain. RFN serves as the native token for the Riffan Rabbit Farms ecosystem, facilitating community participation, governance, and reward distribution within the global rabbit enthusiast community.

The token is designed to create a sustainable digital ecosystem that bridges traditional rabbit breeding with blockchain technology, enabling transparent community engagement and participation in rabbit-related activities.

1. Introduction

Riffan Rabbit Farms, established in 2014 in Lembang, West Java, Indonesia, has been a premier rabbit breeding facility specializing in premium breeds. With nearly a decade of experience, we have developed extensive expertise in rabbit care, breeding, and community building.

The RFN token represents our initiative to extend our physical community into the digital realm, leveraging blockchain technology to create a global ecosystem for rabbit enthusiasts. This document provides comprehensive details about the token's design, functionality, and integration within our ecosystem.

2. Problem Statement

2.1 Fragmented Global Community

Rabbit enthusiasts worldwide lack a unified digital platform for connection, education, and collaboration. Existing communities are fragmented across various social media platforms without integrated functionality for shared activities or governance.

2.2 Limited Participation Mechanisms

Traditional rabbit breeding communities offer limited opportunities for digital participation. Enthusiasts cannot easily engage with breeding programs, educational content, or community decisions without physical presence.

2.3 Funding Sustainability

Animal breeding and conservation initiatives often struggle with sustainable funding models. Traditional donation-based systems lack transparency and consistent engagement mechanisms.

3. Solution: RFN Token Ecosystem

The RFN token ecosystem addresses these challenges through a comprehensive blockchain-based solution:

3.1 Unified Digital Platform

RFN serves as the access token for a unified digital ecosystem where rabbit enthusiasts can connect, share knowledge, and participate in community activities.

3.2 Digital Participation Mechanism

Token holders can participate in various ecosystem activities including breeding program updates, educational content access, and community governance through voting mechanisms.

3.3 Sustainable Reward System

A transparent staking and reward system distributes ecosystem benefits to active participants, creating a sustainable funding model for community initiatives.

6. Staking & Reward Mechanism

6.1 Staking Pool Structure

The staking mechanism allows RFN holders to lock their tokens in various pools to earn rewards from the ecosystem.

Pool Type Lock Period Base APY Reward Token

30-day Lock 30 days 15-25% RFN/BNB
90-day Lock 90 days 30-45% RFN/BNB
180-day Lock 180 days 50-75% RFN/BNB

6.2 Reward Distribution

Rewards are distributed from multiple sources:

  • Initial Rewards Pool: 13.5M RFN allocated for initial staking rewards
  • Ecosystem Buyback: 1% of farm profits allocated to buyback RFN for staking rewards
  • Transaction Fees: Portion of DEX transaction fees redirected to reward pool

Reward Calculation Formula

User Rewards = (User Staked Amount / Total Staked Amount) × Daily Reward Pool

Daily rewards are calculated and distributed automatically through smart contracts.
